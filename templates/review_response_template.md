# 审稿意见回复模板

## 通用回复信结构

```text
[Date]

Dear Editor [Editor's Name] and Reviewers,

Thank you for giving us the opportunity to revise our manuscript entitled "[Manuscript Title]" (Manuscript ID: [ID Number]). We appreciate the constructive comments from the reviewers and the editor, which have helped us improve the quality of our paper significantly.

We have carefully addressed all comments point-by-point below. All changes in the revised manuscript are highlighted in [yellow/blue] for your convenience.

Below is our detailed response to each comment:

---

## Response to Editor's Comments

**Comment 1:** [Copy the editor's comment here]

**Response:** We thank the editor for this valuable suggestion. We have [describe what you did]. This change can be found on page X, line Y.

---

## Response to Reviewer #1

**Comment 1:** [Copy the reviewer's comment here]

**Response:** We appreciate this insightful comment. [Explain your response]. We have added/modified [specific content] in the revised manuscript (Page X, Line Y).

**Comment 2:** [Copy the reviewer's comment here]

**Response:** We agree with the reviewer's concern. [Provide detailed explanation or describe revision]. The relevant text now reads: "[quote the revised text]".

---

## Response to Reviewer #2

[Same format as above]

---

Once again, we thank you and the reviewers for the helpful feedback. We believe the revised manuscript is substantially improved and hope it is now suitable for publication in [Journal Name].

Sincerely,

[Corresponding Author Name]
[Institution]
[Email]
```

---

## 回复策略与技巧

### ✅ 黄金原则

1. **态度诚恳**：即使不同意也要礼貌回应
2. **逐条回复**：不遗漏任何一条意见
3. **清晰标注**：明确说明修改位置
4. **证据充分**：用数据、文献支持你的观点
5. **及时提交**：在截止日期前完成修改

### 🎯 常见场景回复模板

#### 场景 1：完全同意并修改
```
**Response:** We fully agree with this comment. We have [describe action taken]. 
The revised text reads: "[quote new text]" (Page X, Line Y).
```

#### 场景 2：部分同意，需要解释
```
**Response:** We appreciate this suggestion. While we agree that [acknowledge valid point], 
we would like to clarify that [provide explanation]. We have added a sentence to clarify 
this point: "[quote addition]" (Page X, Line Y).
```

#### 场景 3：礼貌地不同意
```
**Response:** We understand the reviewer's concern regarding [issue]. However, 
[provide evidence-based reasoning with references]. We believe that [justify your approach]. 
To avoid confusion, we have added clarification in the manuscript: "[quote addition]" 
(Page X, Line Y).
```

#### 场景 4：需要补充实验/分析
```
**Response:** This is an excellent suggestion. We have conducted additional 
[experiments/analyses] as recommended. The results show that [summarize findings]. 
These new data are presented in [Figure/Table X] and discussed on Page X.
```

### ⚠️ 避免的错误

❌ 情绪化回应或 defensive 语气  
❌ 忽略难以回答的问题  
❌ 只说"已修改"但不说明具体改了什么  
❌ 未标注修改位置  
❌ 回复过于简短缺乏说服力  
❌ 未请合作者校对回复信  

### 📝 检查清单

- [ ] 所有意见都已回复
- [ ] 每条回复都标注了修改位置
- [ ] 语气专业且礼貌
- [ ] 引用了相关文献支持观点
- [ ] 补充的数据/图表已添加
- [ ] 回复信经过所有作者审阅
- [ ] 修改稿中的高亮标记正确
- [ ] 文件格式符合期刊要求

## 附加资源

### 常用表达句式

**表示感谢：**
- We thank the reviewer for this insightful comment.
- We appreciate this valuable suggestion.
- We are grateful for this constructive criticism.

**表示同意：**
- We fully agree with this assessment.
- This is an excellent point, and we have...
- The reviewer is correct that...

**礼貌地解释：**
- We would like to clarify that...
- It is important to note that...
- We respectfully suggest that...

**说明修改：**
- We have revised the text to read: "..."
- This has been clarified in the Methods section.
- Additional data have been added as Figure X.
