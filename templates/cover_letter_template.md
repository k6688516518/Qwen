# Cover Letter 模板

## 通用英文 Cover Letter 模板

```text
[Date]

Dear Editor [Editor's Name if known],

We are pleased to submit our manuscript entitled "[Manuscript Title]" for consideration as a [Article Type, e.g., Research Article/Review] in [Journal Name].

In this study, we [briefly describe the main finding or contribution in 2-3 sentences]. Our work is particularly relevant to [Journal Name]'s readership because [explain relevance to journal scope].

Key highlights of our research include:
• [Highlight 1]
• [Highlight 2]
• [Highlight 3]

We confirm that this manuscript has not been published elsewhere and is not under consideration by another journal. All authors have approved the manuscript and agree with its submission.

We suggest the following potential reviewers (optional):
• [Name, Institution, Email]
• [Name, Institution, Email]

We declare no conflicts of interest.

Thank you for considering our manuscript. We look forward to your response.

Sincerely,

[Corresponding Author Name]
[Title/Position]
[Institution]
[Email]
[Phone Number]
```

## Cover Letter 撰写要点

### ✅ 必含要素
- **论文标题**：准确完整
- **文章类型**：Research Article/Review/Short Communication等
- **核心发现**：用2-3句话概括主要贡献
- **期刊契合度**：说明为什么适合该期刊
- **原创性声明**：未一稿多投
- **作者同意**：所有作者知情同意
- **联系方式**：通讯作者完整信息

### ⚠️ 注意事项
- 控制在1页以内
- 语气专业但不夸张
- 避免简单复制摘要
- 针对特定期刊定制内容
- 检查编辑姓名拼写（如已知）

### 🎯 提升技巧
- 提及期刊近期发表的相关文章
- 强调研究的实际应用价值
- 突出方法学创新或理论突破
- 如有预印本，主动说明
